Logo Overview

Beans and Barley logo is a minimal modern wordmark that depicts Sage qualities in the brand, which is clear, simple and honest. Its design conveys the sense of freshness and calmness and preserves a modern and friendly touch.

Logo Meaning

Clean: simple, minimalistic, modern typography that is not too trustworthy.

Freshness: crisp lines and empty spaces portray wholesome fresh products.

Friendly Typeface: curvy and friendly letterforms produce a pleasing impression.Sage Archetype: real, informed and deliberate aesthetic.

Logo Usage: Clear Space

The width of the free space around the Beans & Barley logo is decided by the height of the grain motif. This should be a minimum area on each side of the logo to ensure that there is no interference to the visual and there is a clear brand image.

Use on Backgrounds

The Beans and Barley logo can be applied on light, dark and dull color backgrounds. Enough contrast should always be provided and this will be necessary to be legible and branded. The inverse or single-color logo is to be utilized when put on colored or dark backgrounds.

Do's

The Beans and Barley logos should only be used in the approved color variations. Make the background and the logo have enough contrast so that there is legibility and clarity.

Dont's

Do not re-color the logo, use unapproved color selections or place the logo on backgrounds that make it less noticeable or change it.

Color's

Fresh foods, cozy dining settings, and a feeling of togetherness served as inspiration for the Beans & Barley color scheme. Rich reds, grain-inspired golds, and neutral hues combine to produce a well-balanced identity that is friendly, contemporary, and reliable.

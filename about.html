Project Information

This project is a rebrand of Beans & Barley, a Milwaukee-based restaurant focused on wholesome, grain-forward food.

The goal was to create a cohesive and modern visual identity that elevates the brand while staying true to the warm, community-driven values it already represents.

Site Description

This website showcases the full rebranding process for Beans & Barley, including research, concept development, and final designs and mockups.

From the logo and color palette to packaging, signage, and social media — this is a complete digital presentation of the project.

About the Company

Beans & Barley is a community-centered restaurant located in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. They bring people together through honest, nourishing food made from quality ingredients.

The brand emphasizes warmth, approachability, and the simple joy of a good meal shared with others. Whether dining in or taking out, the experience feels welcoming and genuine from the very first moment.

Designer Bio

For this rebranding, the goal was to build a visual identity for Beans & Barley that feels warm, consistent, and genuinely reflects who they are from the logo all the way to the packaging.
